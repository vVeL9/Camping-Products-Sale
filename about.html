Who We Are

Founded in 2018, Camping Gear was born from a simple idea: every adventure deserves reliable, high-quality equipment that doesn't break the bank. What started as a small garage operation has grown into a trusted destination for outdoor enthusiasts across the country.

We're not just a store – we're a community of hikers, campers, and nature lovers who believe that the best memories are made under the stars. Every product we sell has been tested by our team in real outdoor conditions.
